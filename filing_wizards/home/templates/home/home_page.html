What is Filing Wizards?

Filing Wizards was created by two ivy-league educated lawyers for the purpose of offering high quality, low cost filing services to small businesses and start-ups. As the owners of a successful small business ourselves, we understand these needs and concerns, and appreciate how important it is to get your business up and running as quickly and cost-efficiently as possible. Whether you are just starting your business, or your business is already underway, we know we can help.

The Three Essential Components of Filing Wizards’ Business Model

Excellent Customer Service

At Filing Wizards, we understand that starting up or modifying your business can be a confusing process. That’s why we’ve dedicated hard work to ensuring that our customer service is the best that’s out there. Please contact us to see for yourself.

Comprehensive Service Packages

At Filing Wizards, we offer a variety of comprehensive service packages and individual services designed to meet your needs. Find the right plan for your business today.

Low Costs - High Quality

Many businesses want a higher level of service than that offered by our online competitors, but are unable to pay the high fees charged by law firms. That’s where Filing Wizards comes in – we’re the middle ground for those looking for improved service at reduced costs.

How to Get Started

Working with Filing Wizards is easy. Go to our services page to select the type of filing service you need, provide your contact information, and one of our representatives will contact you as soon as possible.

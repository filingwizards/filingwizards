Our Service Packages

Select a package or pay as you go!

Below you will find various categories of service we currently offer. Please select the services and/or packages you are interested in and add it to your shopping cart. If you do not see a type of filing service that you need assistance with, simply contact us – we may still be able to help you.

Once you have completed selecting services, simply click “checkout” to finalize your order. At that time, we will ask you to enter additional information relating to billing and other items. Once we have had a chance to review the information provided, one of our Filing Wizards representatives will likely contact you – so please make sure to check your e-mail and/or voicemail and be on the lookout for communications from the team at Filing Wizards. As for billing, please note that you will not be billed until we actually begin the preparation of your filing, and that none of the fees mentioned on this site include additional government fees that will need to be submitted along with your filing.

If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us. In the meanwhile, we look forward to working with you and helping you with your business filing needs.

About Filing Wizards

The Filing Wizards Advantage

Over the course of many years, we have come to realize that many businesses are unable or unwilling to pay the high fees charged by law firms for business filing services. As you probably know from the research you have done, there are a number of different services you can use to help you with the startup of your new business. Ultimately, it is our goal to provide you with the best possible service at the most reasonable cost, whether your business is new or already established. We hope you enjoy the site, and more importantly, our service. Don’t hesitate to reach out feedback if you think we’re not offering an essential filing service, or with suggestions for things that we can do to improve our client’s experiences. Like you, we are interested in constantly trying to improve our business.

Professionalism and Customer Service

Once you have decided to work with us, you will be contacted directly by a Filing Wizards professional with extensive experience in the preparation of business filings. Our professional will then work with you to make sure that the business filing you select is properly formatted and complete according to your input and instruction, and will then submit it to the state of your choosing

Comprehensive Packages

The service packages we offer are comprehensively designed to help you with the preparation of the main business filings you will need to initially get your business up and running initially and in the long-term. None of our competitors do this.

Instead, they offer service packages designed to help you with only the initial filing in the business filing process (i.e., for LLC’s the preparation and submission of the Articles of Organization). Filing Wizards will be there to go the distance with you.

Elimination of Unnecessary Services

We have streamlined our service packages by eliminating those products and services that we believe are unnecessary. In short, at Filing Wizards, you will only pay for what you actually need. In comparison, our competitors regularly include non-essential items that you do not really need, and in the end, we believe you pay for it, whether the cost is explicitly stated or not.

Low Cost

The prices we charge for the service packages we offer take into account the fact that you are a new business looking to keep costs down. It is likely for that reason that you have decided to use Filing Wizards for your business filings, instead of a high priced attorney or accountant. We are mindful of this fact, and have priced our service packages accordingly. You simply won’t find a lower price out there for the types of services and customer service we offer in one of our enhanced packages.

User-Friendly Design

As you navigate around this site, we hope you notice the extra care that we put into making it clear, concise, organized, and most importantly, easy to use. We believe this will help you to find the information and service package you need, quickly and easily.

Beyond that, we believe the straightforward design of this site shows you that quality is important to us. Just because you aren’t paying a lot for our services doesn’t mean you shouldn’t get a great experience. In comparison, our competitors’ sites are often poorly laid out, and cluttered with unnecessary or overly complex information, making it difficult for clients to determine what we offer and what they might need. We hope you will see that with the Filing Wizards’ website, that is not the case.
